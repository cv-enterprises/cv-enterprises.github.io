Projects

Locker Master

Eliminating disputes and piles of paper work caused by the traditional manual locker assignments, Locker Master is able to swiftly take the preferences of the students in account to assign lockers and check for availability on a first come, first served basis.

Community Service Logger

In the quest to earn the 100 hour volunteer medallion, Crescenta Valley students frequently need to turn in forms to confirm hours worked. This application creates accounts and makes it easier to track completed hours for those involved in community service.

Six Million Stories

This is a proof of concept application which enables users to create ‘counters’ that persist over the course of each visit to the site. Each page visit generates a different Holocaust victim story, giving a voice to those lost in time.Built as an educational tool for teachers, this application helps students visualize the enormity of the lives lost in the Holocaust, as well as individualizes the victims. With each page visit, a new Holocaust victim story is presented.

Shared Roof Investments

Partnered with a Durham-based investment firm to offer clients a direct route to ownership of property. This site features all of the intricacies of the business model and allows potential partners to initiate communication with a team of professionals.

Quiz System

Getting rid of tedious scantrons and countless papers, Quiz System opens up the possibility of giving quizzes via online access.

Smoke Tipper

This application equips school administrators with tools to combat the rise of e-cigarettes and vaping.

Injury Tracker

No matter the level, sports injuries are a typical hazard when pursuing athletics. Having over 2500 students enrolled, we decided to create a faster and more efficient way to log, track, and search for student injuries in a database.

Tutor Matching

This application organizes tutor and tutee information, storing names, emails, and ID numbers to aid in tutor matching process.

Virtual Reality

Using the game engines of Unity and Unreal, this project is aimed at the conception and creation of the user interface(UI) for ZPX Interactive Software's latest multiplayer VR game.

Water Notifier

SimpleLayers

Partnered with Simple Layers and the Crescenta Valley Water District in order to convert data from their Excel spreadsheets and paper maps into an integrated mapping system that combines both sources together.
